    The way car buying should be

    Online, on the lot, or a little of both. We offer more ways than ever to shop for your next car, explore your financing options, and sell or trade in your current car.
    Start by browsing the largest used car inventory in the nation. If the one you love isn’t nearby, we can ship it to your closest store or deliver it to you (shipping fee and restrictions may apply).

    Every used car at CarMax comes with:
    • Upfront prices and CarMax Certified quality
    • A 90-Day/4,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Limited Warranty. See store for written details
    • A free vehicle history report
    And every car we sell qualifies for an optional MaxCare extended service plan.

    We also offer quick and flexible financing options. In addition to CarMax Auto Finance, we work with leading financial institutions to give you competitive options. When you’re ready to trade in or sell your car, get our offer online (yes, a real offer in under 2 minutes) that’s good for seven days. We’ll buy your car even if you don’t buy ours.®
    Some participating CarMax stores also offer car shipping as a service. If you need to move one of your cars from one location to another, learn more about Shipping Your Car with CarMax.
